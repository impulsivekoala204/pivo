Pivo Technologies Redesign

CHALLENGE

Here, I decided to turn to UI design instead of UX more broadly. I attempted to redesign the application interface for Pivo Technologies. A fintech interface for truckers in Africa. I used minimal design and a vivid color scheme for the look. I first started with a low fideity wireframe which was ultimately translated to the prototype linked below.
